Welcome to the Carolina's Caregiver & Wellness Hack

The aging population in the United States is growing. And there is a need for innovative Senior care technology.

We have come together to help foster this innovation by creating a Hackathon for Caregivers and Wellness. Teams from Higher education and high schools will come together to compete. This will not only include software, but, can include hardware and 3D printing technologies.
